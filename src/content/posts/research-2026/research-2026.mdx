This spherical joint design was recently developed by researchers at Delft University of Technology in the Netherlands. below is the research publication. This design represents a radical departure for a spherical joint that represents how 3D printing can alter longstanding assumptions of what a thing is. This research may be directly relatable to the flexion systems which must be developed to replace the traditional pivot points of injection molded ski boots.
