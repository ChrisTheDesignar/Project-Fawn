## TL;DR
- **Fold & mesh thinking:** low-poly + origami logic to turn complex curves into **planar tiles** that print strong and assemble fast.  
- **Joinery:** macro-scale, **self-locating edges** that align and **super-jam** under tension—no fiddly dovetails.  
- **Tensioning:** Dyneema paths + braided tubes as **bead-lacing** that cinch the shell and slip when needed.  
- **Scan → flex:** use leg/foot scans and stance data to map **axial stiffness** (side-to-side) vs. **sagittal flex** (for/after).  
- **Sports analogs:** adjacent 3D-printed gear informs materials, lattice, and durability boundaries.
